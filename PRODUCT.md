# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective Tesla buyers and brand admirers browsing on phones, tablets, and desktops. They arrive curious, scanning one product at a time, deciding whether to configure a vehicle or explore energy products. Context is high-intent but low-patience: the page has one fold to make each product feel inevitable.

## Product Purpose

A landing page that presents Tesla's vehicle and energy lineup as a sequence of full-viewport, cinematic product moments. It is a faithful reinterpretation of tesla.com's structure with a darker, more dramatic treatment. Success = the page feels premium, loads fast, reads cleanly on every device, and makes each product the single subject of its screen.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, cinematic. Three words: **engineered, quiet, inevitable.** The voice never oversells; specs and restraint do the talking. Motion conveys craft, not decoration.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (icon-card grids, hero-metric templates, gradient-text headings).
- Busy automotive sites with carousels, badges, and promo clutter.
- The "AI landing page" tells: tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows above every section, numbered `01/02/03` scaffolding, identical card grids.
- Over-animated pages where every section fades in identically.

## Design Principles

1. **One subject per screen.** Each fold is a single product, full-bleed, undistracted.
2. **Monochrome is the identity.** Drama comes from contrast, scrim, and light, never from a color accent.
3. **Motion is choreography, not confetti.** Entrances are orchestrated and earn their place; each reveal fits what it reveals.
4. **Legibility is non-negotiable.** Scrims guarantee text contrast over any image; reduced-motion users get a complete, calm experience.
5. **Specs over adjectives.** Concrete numbers (range, horsepower, price) carry the copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body/large-text contrast verified against scrimmed backgrounds. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` path (no parallax/zoom; gentle or instant transitions). Keyboard-navigable nav and CTAs with visible focus. Semantic landmarks and descriptive image alt text.
